Introduction to Crops

Crops are plants that are cultivated for food, fiber, and other uses. They play a crucial role in agriculture and the economy.

Dependencies on Weather

Crops depend heavily on weather conditions. Factors such as temperature, rainfall, and sunlight affect their growth and yield.

Common Diseases

Crops are susceptible to various diseases caused by pathogens such as fungi, bacteria, and viruses. Common diseases include blight, rust, and mildew.

Cures and Prevention

Prevention and curing of crop diseases involve practices such as crop rotation, use of disease-resistant varieties, and proper use of pesticides.
